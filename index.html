About This Tool

This simple tool was created by Jetmir Troshani.

While working on projects and documentation, he often needed to quickly convert images—such as screenshots, diagrams, or photos of documents—into PDFs. What seemed like a simple task often required extra software or online tools.

To solve this recurring issue, he created a simple tool that does one thing well: convert an image into a PDF in seconds.

Upload the image, click convert, and download the PDF—quick, simple, and practical.
